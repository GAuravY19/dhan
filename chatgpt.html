About Dhansetu

Dhansetu focuses on practical financial planning. We work primarily with salaried individuals and small business owners. Our process is simple. We assess current finances. We design a 3 to 12 month plan. We help implement investments and provide follow-up check-ins.

  2. Risk and goals assessment.
  3. Personalised budget and investment plan.
  4. Implementation support and quarterly review.
